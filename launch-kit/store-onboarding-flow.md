# Store Onboarding Flow

## Goal

Get a new store live with minimum confusion and a working redeem flow.

## Step 1. Qualify the Store

Confirm:

- they are interested in testing a pilot
- they have a staff member who can handle merchant redeem
- they can provide products and images quickly

## Step 2. Collect Store Data

Use [store-intake-template.md](/C:/Users/user/Documents/New%20project/baraka-project/baraka/launch-kit/store-intake-template.md).

Do not start setup before you have:

- store name
- address
- logo or cover image
- at least a small set of products
- merchant contact

## Step 3. Create the Store in Admin

In the admin panel:

- create store
- add description
- add address and visual assets

## Step 4. Add Products

For the first launch:

- keep the assortment small and clear
- focus on strong, visible offers
- avoid overloading the pilot with too many items

Recommended:

- 8 to 20 products
- a mix of best-looking and easy-to-explain offers

## Step 5. Create Merchant Account

In admin:

- create merchant account for the store
- give merchant login and password to the store contact

## Step 6. Test the Flow Yourself

Before store launch, test:

- customer can see the store
- product appears correctly
- activation works
- QR is generated
- merchant can log in
- preview works
- redeem works
- redeem is visible in admin history

## Step 7. Train the Store

Explain only the essentials:

- merchant logs in
- merchant scans or pastes the QR payload
- merchant reviews items
- merchant confirms redeem once

Keep training under 10 minutes.

## Step 8. Go Live

Mark the store ready only after the full flow is tested.

## Step 9. Follow Up

Within 2 to 3 days:

- ask if staff understood the flow
- ask if any customer got confused
- ask if products need adjustment

Then collect formal feedback at the end of the pilot.
