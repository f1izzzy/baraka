# Store Outreach Message

## Full Version

Hello. We are launching Baraka, a Telegram-first platform for local fashion stores.

Customers browse your store and products in a Mini App, activate items they like, and come to the store with a QR code. Your staff confirms the QR in a simple merchant panel, and you can see which offers actually bring people offline.

This is useful for:

- promoting selected products
- moving slow inventory
- testing new offers
- understanding what really converts into store visits

We are currently onboarding a small number of pilot stores. If you're interested, we can show a short demo and connect your store in test mode.
