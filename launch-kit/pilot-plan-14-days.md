# 14-Day Pilot Plan

## Objective

Validate real store usage, customer understanding, merchant adoption, and product-market signal.

## Pilot Structure

- 3 to 5 stores
- 8 to 20 products per store
- 14 days

## Success Metrics

- number of activations
- number of redeems
- activation to redeem conversion
- top-performing stores
- top-performing products
- merchant usability feedback
- customer clarity feedback

## Week 1

### Day 1 to 2

- onboard stores
- upload products
- create merchant accounts
- run internal test flow

### Day 3 to 4

- stores go live
- monitor activation and redeem events
- check admin analytics and audit log

### Day 5 to 7

- gather first merchant feedback
- identify product issues
- adjust bad product titles, prices, or images

## Week 2

### Day 8 to 10

- watch for repeat usage patterns
- identify stores with weak activation
- ask if stronger offers are needed

### Day 11 to 12

- compare stores and products
- note which categories get attention
- note operational friction in merchant flow

### Day 13 to 14

- collect final feedback
- summarize metrics
- decide:
  - continue
  - expand
  - change offer strategy
  - pause and improve product

## Pilot Review Questions

- Which stores generated the most activations?
- Which products converted into redeems?
- Did merchants understand the flow quickly?
- Did customers need help or explanation?
- What blocked better performance?
