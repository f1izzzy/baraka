# Pilot Feedback Form

Use these questions after the store has been live for a few days and again at the end of the pilot.

## For Store Owner or Manager

1. Was the onboarding process clear?
2. Was it easy to provide store and product data?
3. Did Baraka help bring useful customer attention to the store?
4. Which products got the strongest interest?
5. What would make the platform more valuable for your store?

## For Merchant or Staff

1. Was merchant login easy?
2. Was the redeem flow easy to understand?
3. Did any part of scanning or preview feel confusing?
4. Did customers understand the QR process?
5. What should be simpler?

## For Internal Review

1. Did the store launch on time?
2. Did the store complete at least one real redeem?
3. Did admin analytics match what happened in the store?
4. Were there any repeat errors?
5. What should be improved before onboarding the next store?

## Suggested Rating Scale

For each answer, ask for:

- 1 to 5 score
- short explanation

## Final Decision

At the end of the pilot, classify the store as:

- continue
- expand
- improve and retry
- pause
